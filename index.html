About

Remember the last time you reached out to a loved one, family,or friend? It was simply a call away,and that's how amazing technology is. Diana is fascinated by how technology addresses problems in our lives today. She is motivated by her ability to research ,design and code systems that address various problems in our society. She is keen to detail and can collaborate , as she has good communication skills depicted in her personal and group projects such as Blood Teketeke Project alongside her fellow group members in AkiraChix.Her project aims to make blood donation more accessible and manageable through technology and Design. She is a lover of nature therefore, away from her computer, she enjoys travelling ,hiking and meditation.
